About Me

Someone once told me that I'm a unicorn and can do a multitude of things very well. Since I've always loved tech I decided to pursue software engineering! The challenge is rewarding. I'm a junior, but I'm loving it. There is so much I want to create already, to do and explore, my only limitation is I can’t seem to learn fast enough!


    In sum, as a developer, this is me:
  • Tenacious.
  • Team builder. Puzzle solver.
  • Front end skills: JavaScript, React, Redux, React Native, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, Sass
  • Mid-tier: Node.js, Express, Mongoose
  • Back end: MongoDB
  • Cloud: Google

    Reasons I’m a great hire:
  • Integrity matters to me. In a person and in a workplace.
  • Always fun – yet professional – to work with.
  • I’m ready to learn and grow so I can constantly be better than the week before.
  • I’ll tell you my honest opinion – I’m not a ‘yes’ girl just so I get paid or can move on to the next project.
  • If I don’t know how to do something, I figure it out. If I can’t figure it out, I ask for help. That’s part of growing myself and others, and part of being a team.
  • I treat and value others like the one-of-a-kind creation they are.


While becoming a developer, I was fortunate enough to build relationships with a small group of people, find a mentor, and as a team, code together on a daily basis while our mentor continued to push us outside our comfort zones. I am thankful for those relationships as we have continued to grow as a team outside of our schooling and take on projects together. We still code together at least three times a week.
